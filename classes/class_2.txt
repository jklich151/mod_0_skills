Menu Item
Attributes:
1. price - (float)
2. ingredients- (array)
3. outOfStock - (boolean)
4. calories - (integer)

1. increasePrice - (increases the price)
2. listIngredients- (lists the ingredients of the item)
3. removeItem- (removes an item from the menu when ingredients run out)
4. calculateCalories - (adjust calories according to ingredients)
