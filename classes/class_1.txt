Beverages
Attributes:
1. Color (string)
2. isEmpty (boolean)
3. Price (float)
4. Size (string)
Methods:
1. ChangeColor (Changes the color of the drink. Different drinks are different colors)
2. Refill (if true, this will refill the glass and change the boolean to false)
3. PriceDiscount (decrease beverage price by $1.00 during happy hour)
4. OrderSmall (Customer orders a small drink instead of the standard regular size. Decreases size to small.)
